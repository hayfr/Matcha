Matcha Latte

Literally just Matcha green tea but the matcha powder is whisked into warm milk instead.

How to Make Matcha Latte:

  • Whisk 1 tsp matcha with ~2 oz hot water.
  • Heat and froth ~6 oz milk.
  • Combine milk and matcha in a cup
  • Add honey or brown suger as a sweetener if wanted
